ABOUT US
01

Metabeat is a NFT based F2E (Fan To Earn) Platform, where creators / artists and fans grow as partners through engagements and rewards.

With a growing number of NFT creations and users on platform, the partnerships between creators / artists and fans strengthen through the positive experience of symbiosis fueled by Metabeat ecosystem that reflects values and contributions by users in Metabeat communities.

Metabeat ecosystem places users as direct beneficiaries by tokenizing user contributions and their social influence through personal networks.
